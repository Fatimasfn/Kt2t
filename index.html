Katsina State was created in 1987 from the northern half of Kaduna State.

kingdom and emirates

Katsina was founded in the 10th or 11th century as one of the Hausa Bakwai, or "Seven True Hausa States". Islam was introduced in the 1450s, and the state became prosperous after camel caravans crossed the Sahara. In 1513, the Songhai conquered Katsina.

Location

Katsina State is bordered by the Niger Republic to the north, and by the Nigerian states of Jigawa and Kano to the east, Kaduna to the south, and Zamfara to the west.
